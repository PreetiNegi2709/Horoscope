LIBRA

Typical Libra personality traits

Diplomatic (read: shape shifters)

Libra strive for everything to be smooth-sailing and plain seas, and can pour oil on troubled waters, build bridges, and mend fences like a pro-diplomat.
That said, all their empathy, listening and deep-down understanding can mean their own opinions and desires get lost in the wash, indeed sometimes they may not even really know their own mind. You may have Libran pals whose company you love, but whose values and opinions you couldn’t, if pressured, articulate. Nor could they (though they’ll know yours).

Great listener (read: a bit of a gossip)

Air signs love information, news and education. They soak up the world around them like a big brainy sponge. Librans are no exception, but what they tend to do with this information represents a conflict in their character: the urge and need to gossip. On the one hand, they kinda know it’s going to backfire and they hate to be disliked and they abhor conflict and confrontations. BUT, on the other hand, that’s all in a hypothetical distant future. Right here, right now, the easiest way to impress/humour/cajole the person in front of them is to drop a juicy goss-bomb. So that is what they do. Can’t help it nu-uh.

A Weigher-upper (read: entitled)

Librans strive for fairness and justice constantly, and they love the law. They deconstruct every situation with their little legal eagle brain and pick over the debris, organising right from wrong. However, they're often biased in their own favour. So, when they’re carefully, painstakingly explaining why they should take the bigger room, in their own mind, it DOES make sense. To everyone else? Nope.

Idealist (read: indecisive)

It KILLS Libra to fully, 100% commit to something if there’s more than one option - even if it’s something as innocuous as a milkshake flavour. They always want the best experience and they believe, through logic and rationale and analysis, they can actually create ‘perfect pathways’ through life, sidestepping dodgy meals, hop-skipping queues.. This nitpicking over-thinking of every thing is apt to drive partners and friends/family of Librans up the wall.

Attractive Libra traits

Librans are ruled by Venus, the goddess of love, so they adore beautiful things and culture, and seek perfect balance and harmony in all areas of their life. Librans are one of the most charming, beautiful and flirtatious sign. If you know one, chances are you’re attracted to them! Attractive traits include:

    Their Venus planetary ruler gives them a head-start when it comes to flirting and emitting sexy signals.
    They know just the right thing to say at the right time, they have effortless grace and tact.
    They love smiling! Librans always look pleasant and serene (even when they’re NOT).
